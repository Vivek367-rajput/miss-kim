Frequently Asked Questions

How do I invite AwesomeBot to my server?

+
Click the "Invite Bot" button at the top of the page, authorize the bot, select your server, and you're done! The bot will be added to your server immediately.

Is AwesomeBot free to use?

+
Yes! AwesomeBot is completely free. We offer a premium tier with additional features, but all core features are free forever.

How do I set up the welcome message?

+
Use the dashboard to configure your server settings. Go to the "Welcome" section and customize your welcome message. You can use variables like {user} and {server}.

Can I customize the bot prefix?

+
Yes! You can change the bot prefix in your server settings through the dashboard. Use any character or string you prefer.

How do I get support?

+
You can join our support server, contact us via email, or check out our documentation. Our support team is always ready to help!

Is there a premium version?

+
Yes! Premium members get access to advanced features, priority support, and exclusive commands. Check the dashboard for premium features.
